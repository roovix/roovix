Introduction

Roovix is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data responsibly and securely.

Data We Collect

  • Auth: Firebase Authentication handles secure login and account credentials.
  • Profile Info: We collect your name, email, and profile photo.
  • Content: Plain text stored using Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Media: Profile photos or uploads are stored securely on Cloudinary.

How We Use Data

Your information is used to provide, secure, and improve Roovix services. We do not sell or misuse any personal data.

We process your data to deliver the services you've requested, enhance your experience, and maintain the security of our platform. All processing is done with strict adherence to data protection principles.

Data Sharing & Security

We never sell your data. We only share data with trusted services (Firebase, Cloudinary) under secure, encrypted channels.

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols both during transmission and at rest. We regularly review and update our security practices to ensure the highest level of protection.

Your Rights

You can view, update, or request deletion of your data. We honor privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.

Under these regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: Auth and database infrastructure
  • Cloudinary: File and image storage

These services have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data. We carefully select partners who maintain high standards of data protection.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, reach out at support@roovix.com.

For privacy-specific inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please include "Privacy Request" in your subject line.
