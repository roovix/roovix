Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email
How to Reset Your Password
How can I enable two-factor authentication?

To enable two-factor authentication:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to Account Settings > Security
  3. Click on "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  4. Follow the setup instructions

We recommend using an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy for the most secure experience.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can change your email address in your account settings. For security reasons, we'll send a verification link to both your old and new email addresses to confirm the change.

Privacy Concerns

How is my data protected?

We take data protection seriously. Your data is protected through:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • Compliance with global data protection regulations
Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes, you can request deletion of your personal data at any time. Go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete My Data to initiate the process. Please note that some data may be retained for legal and compliance purposes, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Does Roovix share my data with third parties?

We only share your data with third parties in specific circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us deliver our services
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With your explicit consent

We never sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

Billing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfers (for annual enterprise plans)
  • Google Pay and Apple Pay
How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to Account Settings > Billing
  3. Click on "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

Please note that cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 14 days, you can request a full refund. For refund requests after this period, please contact our support team to discuss your specific situation.

Technical Support

How do I contact technical support?

You can contact our technical support team through multiple channels:

  • Email: support@roovix.com
  • Live chat: Available on our website 24/7
  • Phone: +1-800-ROOVIX (Business hours: 9am-5pm EST)
  • Help Center: Browse our knowledge base at help.roovix.com
What are the system requirements?

For optimal performance, we recommend:

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - latest versions)
  • Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
  • For desktop applications: Windows 10/11, macOS 10.15+, or Ubuntu 20.04+
  • For mobile apps: iOS 14+ or Android 10+
Is there an API available?

Yes, we offer a comprehensive API for developers. Our API allows you to integrate Roovix services into your own applications and workflows. Documentation is available at developers.roovix.com. API access is included in Business and Enterprise plans.
